Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

DashRx only collects business contact information you voluntarily submit through our quote request form, including:

  • Pharmacy name and address
  • Contact person name
  • Business phone number and email address
  • Estimated monthly prescription volume
  • Additional notes about your delivery needs

We do not collect any patient information or protected health information (PHI).

How We Use Your Information

We use the business contact information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request with service details and pricing
  • Contact you about potential partnership opportunities
  • Provide customer support for our delivery services

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your business information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

We retain your business contact information for up to 12 months to allow for quote follow-up and potential partnership discussions. After this period, information is securely deleted from our systems.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we have about your pharmacy
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information from our systems
  • Opt out of future communications

Analytics and Cookies

Our website may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Plausible) that do not track personal information or use cookies. These tools help us understand website usage patterns without compromising your privacy.
